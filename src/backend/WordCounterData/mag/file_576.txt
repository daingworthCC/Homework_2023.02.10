4113041 Three ways to bring global flavor to a rotisserie bird. <p> Spanish Chicken Paella <p> SERVES 6 In a deep skillet over medium heat, heat 2 tsp oil. Add 3 oz chorizo, sliced in 1/4-inch rounds, and saut 4 minutes. Add 1 diced onion and saut until softened, about 2 minutes. Add 2 minced garlic cloves and saut 1 minute more. Stir in 1 tsp paprika, 3/4 cup canned diced tomatoes, drained, and 13/4 cups water, and bring to a boil. Stir in one 8-oz package yellow rice mix, simmer 1 minute, and cover. Reduce heat to medium-low and cook 20 minutes. Place 8 pieces of cooked chicken on top of rice. Sprinkle 1/2 cup frozen peas over top, cover, and cook 5 minutes more. Top with parsley and pimiento strips. <p> Moroccan Chicken <p> SERVES 4 In a skillet over medium heat, heat 2 Tbsp oil. Add 1 onion, finely diced, and saut until golden, about 5 minutes.
