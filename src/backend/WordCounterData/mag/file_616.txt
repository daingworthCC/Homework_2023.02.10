4143641 Section : NewsDISPATCHES # Bloomsbury Publishing has completed a two-year project to digitize the Sir Winston Churchill Archive www.churchillarchive.com, a trove of nearly 1 million documents housed at Churchill College in Cambridge, England. The collection -- which includes drafts of the British prime minister 's wartime speeches, official letters, personal correspondence and even cigar invoices -- is now available to libraries and grade schools for a sliding fee. Subscriptions are not yet available for individual researchers. # PHOTO( BLACK &amp; WHITE) #
