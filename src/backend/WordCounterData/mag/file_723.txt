4155541 Section : DEPARTMENTS : 30 MINUTES Toasted Quinoa with Crunchy Pumpkin, Cilantro, and Lime-Sumac Dressing # SERVES 4 30 MINUTES OR LESS # " This salad is very loosely based on classic Middle Eastern tabbouleh. We came up with the crunchy pumpkin topping after frying onions for mujadara, another Lebanese favorite, " says author Lucy Malouf. " Shredded pumpkin, like the onion, has lots of natural sugars, and it crisps up beautifully when you fry it. " She suggests serving with griddled fl at breads and a couple of tasty dips. # SALAD # 1 cup quinoa # 1 cups low-sodium vegetable broth or water # 1 cup peeled, grated fresh pumpkin # 1 cup vegetable oil # tsp. ground cumin # tsp. sea salt # 1 cups cilantro, shredded # 1 tomato, diced( cup) # 3 green onions sliced( cup) # 1 tsp.
