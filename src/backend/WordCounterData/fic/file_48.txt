1007641 A CHEROKEE FOLK TALE : why the opossum 's tail is bare <p> Many, many years ago, the Possum had a long, bushy tail. He was so proud of it that he combed it every morning. He sang about it whenever the animals held a dance. The Rabbit, who had no tail since the Bear pulled it out, became very jealous. She made up her mind to play a trick on the Possum. <p> A great council meeting had been planned. There was to be a dance and all the animals were to be present. It was the Rabbit 's task to send out the news to everyone. As the Rabbit was passing Possum 's house, she stopped to ask him whether he would be at the dance. The Possum said yes, but he would come only if he could have a special seat. <p> " I have such a handsome tail, " he said. " I ought to sit where everybody can see me.
