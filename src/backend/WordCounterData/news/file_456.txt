4060041 Now that the holiday season is officially here, a flurry of seasonal family activities has hit the Chicago area. <p> One of the biggest annual events is the Bank of America Winter WonderFest, running Dec. 5 through Jan. 4 at Navy Pier. Now in its eighth year, the fest is preceded by Santa 's arrival by boat on Dock Street at 5 p.m. Nov. 29. Following a welcoming fireworks display and the lighting of Dock Street, Santa will proceed to the Family Pavilion Stage, where he 'll be in residence granting wishes and posing for photos through Dec. 24. <p> Delores Robinson, assistant director of public relations for the Pier, says Winter WonderFest is " like opening a gift all day long " because there is so much for families to do. And this year, she said, families can enjoy reduced wristband prices. <p> " We want families to know they can still come to Navy Pier and have a great time and get a great deal, and that Navy Pier still provides great rides and attractions, " she said. @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ Wonder Obstacle Course.
