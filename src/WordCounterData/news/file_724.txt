4194441 <h> Cops warn of 5 robberies in 3 hours in Austin neighborhood <p> Armed robbers committed five robberies within a little more than three hours one morning last week in the Austin neighborhood, police warned in an alert Monday. <p> The two men approached people who were about to get into their cars or already were in their cars, showed handguns and told the victims they were being robbed, police said in the alert. The robbers took property such as wallets and cellphones but did not steal vehicles, police said. <p> All the robberies happened between 4:15 a.m. and 7:30 a.m. Thursday in the Austin Police District, according to the alert. <p> At 7:30 a.m. in the 5800 block of West Race Avenue ; <p> At 6:45 a.m. in the 5800 block of West Erie Street ; <p> At 5 a.m. in the 1000 block of North Mayfield Avenue ; <p> About 4:20 a.m. in the 5000 block of West Ohio Street ; <p> About 4:15 a.m. in the 500 block of North LeClaire Avenue. <p> The robbers were described as black men, ages 18 to 25 @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ Clothing descriptions were not released. <p> Anyone with information about the robbers is asked to call Area North detectives at 312-744-8263.
