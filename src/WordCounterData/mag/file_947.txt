5014641 <h> How to make a delicious and healthy frozen fruit pop <p> Some prepackaged pops might be calorie-friendly, but many have dyes and preservatives. Making your own is easy, and you can use gut-friendly ingredients like kefir( a cultured dairy drink), fresh fruit and dark chocolate. <p> Think of kefir as a cousin of yogurt with a similarly tangy taste. It 's high in protein and loaded with probiotic cultures, up to 11 different strains compared to the one or two found in most yogurts. It 's great in frozen pop recipes because the freezing does n't kill the beneficial cultures. Also, its texture is a bit thinner than yogurt, which makes it ideal for freezing with berries and other healthy add-ins, like naturally sugar-free cacao nibs. <p> Nibs are cracked whole cocoa beans. They 're high in antioxidants plus they give you a good dose of fiber and a pleasant crunch. You can usually find them in health food stores or order them online.
